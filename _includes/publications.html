Contact Sensing via Joint Torque Sensors and a Force/Torque Sensor for Legged Robots
J. Grinberg, Y. Ding
IEEE CASE 2025, Los Angeles, CA
[PDF] / [DOI] / [Slides]

We introduce a low-cost distributed joint torque sensing framework combined with a hip-mounted FT sensor for whole-body contact detection in legged robots. Simulation and hardware validation demonstrate sub-centimeter localization accuracy and force errors < 0.2 N.

3D Steering and Localization in Pipes and Burrows Using an Externally Steered Soft Growing Robot
Y. Qin, J. Grinberg, W. Heap, A. Okamura
Under review. ArXiv preprint.
[arXiv] / [PDF]

We develop an external steering device for actuating vine robots, capable of steering and localizing in complex 3D environments such as pipes and animal burrows, validated through controlled experiments and field tests.

Design and Modeling of a Compact Spooling Mechanism for the COAST Guidewire Robot
T. A. Brumfiel, J. Grinberg, B. Siopongco, J. P. Desai
IEEE Robotics and Automation Letters (RA-L), Oct. 2024
[PDF] / [DOI]

We present the design and analysis of a compact, high-fidelity spooling mechanism for guidewire actuation in minimally invasive surgical robotics.
