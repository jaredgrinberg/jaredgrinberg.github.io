Selected Projects
ROB550 Armlab — Vision-Guided Block Manipulation

Developed forward and inverse kinematics for a 5-DOF robotic arm integrated with an overhead RGB-D camera. Implemented block detection and classification from top-down vision, and built a GUI that allowed click-to-pick-and-place block manipulation.

Team members: Zhongxiao Deng, Chankyo Kim, Hoang Nyugen

ROB550 Botlab — SLAM and Autonomous Navigation

Programmed a differential-drive mobile robot (MBot platform) with lidar, IMU, and wheel encoders to perform SLAM and Monte Carlo Localization. Built maze maps successfully and implemented A* path planning with feedback control. Demonstrated autonomous navigation, though localization error led to some trajectory drift.

Team members: Sacchin Sundar, Longzhen Yuan

Pill Nanny — Automated Pill Dispenser

Designed and prototyped an automated pill dispenser for neurodivergent individuals (ADHD and Autism). System included customizable reminders and reward-based adherence tracking. Demonstration video shows mobile app interface on the left and physical pill dispensing of varying pill types, sizes, and quantities on the right.

Team members: Julien Franchet-Schaer, Michel Maalouli, Marc Nassar, Monique Tabet, Kaden Stillwagon

4-DOF Robot — Pre-Planned Circular Trajectory

Designed and built a 4-DOF robotic arm to trace a circular trajectory using the Denavit-Hartenberg procedure for kinematic modeling. Simulation results in MATLAB validated the planned trajectories before hardware implementation.

Team members: Richard Barrezueta, Andre Brown, Jesse DeMatteo, Ojas Rade
